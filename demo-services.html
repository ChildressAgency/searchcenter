Services

We Get Results

Our end-to-end Search Management System (SMS) integrates the search process from initial search request to invoice delivery to follow-up customer feedback. Our system continues to provide Search-Center with an advantage in administrative efficiency and Analyst effectiveness - quite simply, Search-Center delivers the highest value product in the industry.

How it Works

Search Inquiry

Our team of experts takes the time to discuss your search needs. Upon initial contact with Search-Center, our staff will immediately inquire about the main technology area of your search request and route your call to the appropriate Director.

Each of our Directors is a licensed patent attorney. We always take the time to discuss the search and particularly, any specific insights expected to be gained from the results.

Review and Plan

Our Directors will immediately review the search materials - e.g., an invention disclosure, a patent, a product description, etc. - to create the most appropriate plan of action.

The Director will then draft, for customer approval, a set of proposed search features - similar to a broad claim and a number of dependent claims. This step ensures proper search scope and also sets a foundation for the Analyst to execute the highest quality search.

Customer Approval

We will work with you to make sure your search needs are met and are met on time.

Each estimate will additionally detail in particular where we will search and when the results will be delivered. We never charge an expediting fee. If you have a deadline, please let us know - we are always eager to accommodate.

We Deliver

Our mission will always be to deliver the highest quality results possible. We also believe additional value may be achieved through the manner in which information is presented. At Search-Center, our standard deliverable is a feature matrix.

Along with the feature matrix, your results will also include a database with a bibliography of Central and Peripheral references, a complete search history, and a histogram of the top 10 CPC subclasses.
