About

Who We Are

Search-Center provides high quality patent searching services to the worldwide intellectual property community. Our customers include patent attorneys and agents, inventors, executives, engineers, R&D teams, academia, start-ups and investment firms.

Our missions will always be to deliver the highest quality results possible. From initial search request to invoice delivery to follow-up customer feedback, we strive to deliver the highest value product in the industry. And we will never outsource your search. Our Analysts reside and work exclusively in the US.
